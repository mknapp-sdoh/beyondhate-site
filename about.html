About

Michael Knapp, PhD, builds technology for mission-driven organizations. He has spent the past two years exploring how AI can create value for nonprofits and social enterprises—and learning that the most valuable outcomes are often the ones you didn't plan for.

This work on hate speech and vaccine hesitancy grew from a broader interest in how emerging technology can support behavior change at scale, in domains where information alone isn't enough.

Michael is founder of sdoh.org and author of Seedwork, a methodology for building with LLMs.

Related projects

  • sdoh.org — Professional hub and recommendation engine (coming soon)
